Why I'm learning to code...

I was first interested in making web-sites in 6th and 7th grade and began by teaching myself HTML and a little VB––then I moved to California and started skateboarding and got distracted for 15 years. During that time I also became a musician and more recently have become obsessed with photography.

Coding has the potential to work collaboratively with any one of my other pursuits/passions and that's the great thing about it. This technology is not a means to its own end, but rather tools to expand the possibilities of any other endeavor in the world.
